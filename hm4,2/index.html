Zoro

Zoro is the first crewmate to join Monkey D. Luffy to be part of his crew of pirates, after he is rescued by Luffy from execution. Zoro is a highly skilled swordsman and serves as the crew's combatant, though he possesses an extremely poor sense of direction which recurs as a running gag throughout the series.

Nami

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She joined the crew in the Orange Town arc, but she left during the Baratie arc. After Luffy defeated Arlong, Nami rejoined the crew, and ever since she has become an important character.

Shanks

Shanks possesses the ability to use Kenbunshoku Haki, as revealed in his vivre card and has almost unrivalled mastery over it. He is known for being the Killer of Observation Haki as he is able to stop people from being able to use it as seen from Kidd.

Sanji

Sanji was the third prince of the Germa Kingdom before being banished by his father, Vinsmoke Judge. Sanji's siblings are all genetically modified super-soldiers, but, due to Sanji's mother taking precautionary actions, he was born without the superhuman abilities of his siblings.

Usopp

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates. He joined the crew after the Syrup Village arc. Usopp is a known coward, but he wants to become a brave warrior like his dad, Yasopp, who is one of the strongest characters in One Piece. At times, Usopp has put his body on the line to prove his resolution.
